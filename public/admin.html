Carry-Fwd A — Leftover days from previous year. Max 7 days. Expires 31 March.
Current Yr B — This year's AL allocation set by admin.
Used A / Used B — Days already consumed from each bucket. A is deducted first.
Balance — Total remaining days (A + B minus used). Does not account for March 31 expiry of A.
Records are auto-created when an employee first checks their balance. Delete removes the record for that year only.
